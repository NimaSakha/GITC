GITC is offering deep technological and programming courses co-funded by Fund for Armenian Relief. GITC courses are available both offline and online. GITC is also collaborating with international organizations and private partners implementing various charity educational programs in all the regions of Armenia.
Key programming courses last 6 months combining theoretical and practical ways of teaching. In general students have a 6 hour load per week per course.
If you want to be part of life-changing projects and have your contribution in empowering Armenian people through tech education and employment you are welcome to donate through our website. In case you want to use other methods of donations, please kindly email to info@gitc.am.
Our trainers are representing the most significant technological companies in Armenia with more than 7 years of experience.
GITC graduates are introduced to tech companies for internship opportunities. We are actively collaborating with tech companies to promote the employment of our successful graduates.
Our instructors come from top technological companies and bring their working and technical skillset to share with our students. Our trainers are deeply involved in supporting with studentsâ€™ internships upon graduation.
Armenian people are facing huge unemployment problems, yet the technological sector is the only sector in Armenia where job offers are more than available experts. It is significant mentioning that private companies offering programming courses are too expensive for the regional people, while GITC offers affordable conditions thanks to its donors.

GITC educates and supports war veterans, family members of martyred soldiers and single mothers helping them with employment.
