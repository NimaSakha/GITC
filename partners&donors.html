Committed partners and donors

GITC is grateful for the generous support it receives from individuals, foundations and partners. Our donors make it possible for our Foundation to develop the strategic insights and solutions that help children, youth, women, veterans in the regions of Armenia toward changing life quality, raising the feeling of importance, creating educational and employment opportunities.

Supporters engage with the work of GITC through partnerships, sponsorships, and charitable donations.

GITC gives special thanks to the Fund for Armenian Relief for continuous and outstanding generosity. To learn more, visit www.farusa.org

To all our donors, thank you for contributing to our success. Please note that we have made every attempt to correctly recognize our generous donors.
